Implantology

Overview

Implantology is an emerging super speciality in dentistry functioning as an independent department with specialists from co-specialties. In addition to conducting a one-year Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences certificate programme, the department provides permanent solutions for prematurely lost teeth.

The department is fully equipped with the latest implant surgical kits, physio dispenser systems, and advanced surgical equipment, ensuring high standards of patient care and clinical excellence.

Patient Services

  • Single and Multiple Tooth Replacement
  • Implant Supported Dentures
  • All-on Concept Full Arch Implants
  • Full Mouth Rehabilitation
  • Sinus Lift Procedures & Zygomatic Implants
