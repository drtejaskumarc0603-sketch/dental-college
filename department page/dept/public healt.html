Public Health Dentistry

Overview

The Department of Public Health Dentistry is dedicated to meeting the oral health needs of communities that lack access to dental care. The primary objective is to bring dental health awareness and services to the larger population through outreach initiatives.

Through rural dental camps and adopted satellite centres, the department educates communities using charts, audio-visual aids, and awareness programs. It also provides dental treatment and referrals to specialized clinics whenever required.

Students gain first-hand experience at the grassroots level, developing clinical skills and a strong sense of social responsibility by working with underserved communities.

The department operates a modern mobile dental clinic that reaches remote areas, along with four satellite centres providing continuous dental care to surrounding populations.

Achievements include university ranks by postgraduate students and the Best P.G. Award won by Dr. Amith Kumar (2013, National Level). Faculty members have also contributed to several national and international publications.

Future Plans: Collaborations with international NGOs, organizing postgraduate conventions, and hosting national conferences.
