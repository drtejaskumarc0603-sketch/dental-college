Research & Publication

Overview

The institution boasts of having an active Research & Publication Department that conducts several research projects by both staff and students. The department is equipped with advanced facilities including a Real Time PCR, which attracts students from various colleges to undertake research work.

It also publishes an indexed scientific journal titled “Journal of Health Sciences and Research”, contributing significantly to academic advancement and innovation in dental sciences.

Recognitions

  • NAAC ‘A’ Grade Accreditation
  • ISO – 9001: 2008 Certification
  • IAO (International Accreditation Organization – U.S.A.) Accreditation
  • Recognition by Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Glasgow, U.K. (Part I & II MFDS Examination)
