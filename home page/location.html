RajaRajeswari Dental College & Hospital

RajaRajeswari Dental College & Hospital is situated on a sprawling 5-acre campus along the Bangalore–Mysore Highway, approximately 19 km from Vidhana Soudha.

The lush green campus features over 4 lakh sq. ft. of modern infrastructure, housing the dental institute, 10 specialty clinics, advanced laboratories, classrooms, and residential facilities for staff and students.

The location ensures easy accessibility while providing a calm and professional healthcare environment.
